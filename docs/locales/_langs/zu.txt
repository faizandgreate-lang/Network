Ngqala ngomsebenzi wezobuchwepheshe nekhompyutha, ngilungisa izinkinga futhi ngiphatha izinhlelo. Kamuva ngahola imisebenzi yabasebenzi neyephrojekthi e-Saudi Arabia — ukuphathwa kwezihlala, ezempilo, nezindawo zezinkampani eziningi.
