Network Monitor — Quick Guide
Creator: Mohammad Faizan Khan
Websites: https://linux-aios.com  |  https://khan.linux-aios.com

=== START ===
1. Double-click START.command or Network Monitor.app
2. Keep Terminal open
3. Open http://127.0.0.1:5080/ in your browser

=== PAGES ===
Home — tutorial and about the creator
Device list — scan Wi-Fi and LAN, see all devices
Network map — flowchart from your last scan

=== SCAN ===
Click "Scan Wi-Fi + LAN" on the Device list page.
Wait about 1–2 minutes. Empty fields mean not detected.
